**Module 4: Streamlining Legal Document Drafting**

**Module Goal:** To enable legal professionals to leverage BetterCallClaude for efficient and accurate legal document drafting, from initial creation to final review. This module will cover the use of the `/legal:draft` command, specialized drafting agents, and compliance review tools to produce high-quality, professional legal documents.

---

**Lesson 1: The `/legal:draft` Command**

*   **Learning Objectives:**
    *   Draft a variety of legal documents, including contracts and court submissions, using the `/legal:draft` command.
    *   Utilize the `Legal Drafter` persona for consistent, high-quality, and context-aware drafting.
    *   Customize and adapt document templates and specific clauses to fit individual case needs.

*   **Key Concepts:**
    *   The `/legal:draft` command
    *   `Legal Drafter` persona
    *   Template-based drafting
    *   Clause customization
    *   Document structure and formatting
    *   Multi-lingual document generation

*   **Lesson Plan:**
    *   **(1) Introduction to the `/legal:draft` Command and `Legal Drafter` Persona (15 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Introduce the `/legal:draft` command as the primary tool for generating legal documents. Explain how the `Legal Drafter` persona ensures that the output adheres to Swiss legal standards and stylistic conventions.
        *   **Demonstration:** Show a simple drafting task, such as creating a standard non-disclosure agreement (NDA), to illustrate the basic functionality.

    *   **(2) Drafting Contracts, Court Submissions, and Other Legal Documents (20 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Cover the drafting of various document types. For contracts, explain how to specify parties, terms, and conditions. For court submissions, demonstrate how to structure arguments, cite precedents, and format the document for the relevant court.
        *   **Activity: "Drafting Workshop":** Provide learners with a scenario requiring a simple contract (e.g., a service agreement). Guide them through using the `/legal:draft` command to generate the document, specifying key terms and clauses.

    *   **(3) Customizing Document Templates and Clauses (20 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Explain how to modify BetterCallClaude's default templates and clauses to meet specific client or case requirements. This includes adding, removing, or editing sections of a document.
        *   **Activity: "Template Modification Exercise":** Have learners take a standard contract template generated by BetterCallClaude and modify it to include a unique clause based on a hypothetical client request. Review and discuss the results.

**Lesson 2: Specialized Document Drafting**

*   **Learning Objectives:**
    *   Draft complex corporate documents for M&A and governance with the `Corporate & Commercial` agent.
    *   Manage and draft documents for real estate transactions using the `Real Estate` agent.
    *   Prepare fiscal and tax-related documents with the assistance of the `Fiscal Legal Expert`.

*   **Key Concepts:**
    *   `Corporate & Commercial` agent
    *   `Real Estate` agent
    *   `Fiscal Legal Expert`
    *   Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) documentation
    *   Corporate governance documents
    *   Real estate purchase agreements
    *   Tax filings and fiscal reports

*   **Lesson Plan:**
    *   **(1) Drafting Corporate Documents with the `Corporate & Commercial` Agent (20 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Introduce the `Corporate & Commercial` agent and its capabilities in handling complex business law documents. Cover examples such as share purchase agreements, articles of association, and board resolutions.
        *   **Demonstration:** Showcase the drafting of a set of documents for a simple M&A transaction, highlighting the agent's ability to maintain consistency across related documents.

    *   **(2) Handling Real Estate Transactions with the `Real Estate` Agent (15 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Explain how the `Real Estate` agent can streamline the drafting of documents for property transactions, including purchase agreements, lease agreements, and land registry applications. Discuss the importance of cantonal-specific real estate laws.
        *   **Activity: "Real Estate Scenario":** Give learners a scenario involving a property sale and have them use the `Real Estate` agent to generate a draft purchase agreement for a specific canton.

    *   **(3) Preparing Fiscal and Tax Documents with the `Fiscal Legal Expert` (20 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Introduce the `Fiscal Legal Expert` and its role in preparing tax-related documents. This includes tax rulings, objections to tax assessments, and fiscal due diligence reports. Emphasize the agent's knowledge of Swiss federal and cantonal tax laws.
        *   **Demonstration:** Walk through the process of using the `Fiscal Legal Expert` to prepare a response to a tax authority's request for information, ensuring all legal and formal requirements are met.

**Lesson 3: Document Review & Analysis**

*   **Learning Objectives:**
    *   Use the `Data Protection Specialist` to review documents for GDPR and Swiss nDSG/FADP compliance.
    *   Leverage the `Compliance Officer` for comprehensive FINMA and AML/KYC checks.
    *   Analyze sensitive documents in a secure, privacy-preserving manner using the local Ollama LLM.

*   **Key Concepts:**
    *   `Data Protection Specialist`
    *   `Compliance Officer`
    *   GDPR and nDSG/FADP compliance
    *   FINMA and AML/KYC regulations
    *   Privacy-preserving document analysis
    *   Local LLM (Ollama)
    *   Anonymization techniques

*   **Lesson Plan:**
    *   **(1) Document Review for Data Protection with the `Data Protection Specialist` (20 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Introduce the `Data Protection Specialist` and its function in identifying and flagging potential data protection issues in documents. Explain the key requirements of GDPR and the Swiss nDSG/FADP.
        *   **Activity: "Data Protection Audit":** Provide learners with a sample document containing personal data. Have them use the `Data Protection Specialist` to conduct a compliance review and generate a report of findings and recommendations.

    *   **(2) Leveraging the `Compliance Officer` for FINMA and AML/KYC Checks (20 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Explain the role of the `Compliance Officer` in ensuring that documents and processes adhere to Swiss financial regulations, including FINMA circulars and anti-money laundering (AML) laws.
        *   **Demonstration:** Showcase how the `Compliance Officer` can scan a client onboarding file for AML/KYC red flags and generate a compliance checklist.

    *   **(3) Privacy-Preserving Document Analysis with Local LLM (15 minutes):**
        *   **Presentation:** Revisit the concept of the local Ollama LLM introduced in Module 1. Explain in greater detail how to use this feature for analyzing highly sensitive documents without data ever leaving the user's machine. Discuss the importance of anonymization as an additional layer of protection.
        *   **Activity: "Secure Review Workflow":** Guide learners through a complete privacy-preserving workflow: (1) Anonymize a sample confidential document. (2) Use BetterCallClaude with the local Ollama LLM to analyze the document for specific legal issues. (3) Discuss the benefits and limitations of this approach.
