## Legal Issues and Statutory Framework

### Key Questions (Fragestellungen)

1. Is the retroactive probation extension valid? (OR 335b — probation must be agreed at start; extensions require written agreement before expiry)
2. Does the termination constitute retaliatory dismissal after a compliance report? (OR 336 lit. d — missbräuchliche Kündigung wegen Geltendmachung von Ansprüchen nach Treu und Glauben)
3. What is the correct notice period — regular or probation? (OR 335a / 335c — with void probation extension, 2-month ordinary notice applies)
4. Does a protection period (Sperrfrist) apply? (OR 336c — timing restrictions on termination)
5. What is the indemnity calculation and ceiling? (OR 336a II — up to 6 months' salary, court discretion within range)
6. Which court has jurisdiction and which procedure applies? (Arbeitsgericht Zürich, Art. 114 lit. c ZPO — court fee exemption threshold at CHF 30,000)
