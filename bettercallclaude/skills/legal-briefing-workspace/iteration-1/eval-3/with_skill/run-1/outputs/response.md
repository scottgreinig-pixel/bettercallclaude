Yes, **BGE 141 III 569** is correctly formatted.

This is a valid Swiss Federal Supreme Court (Bundesgericht) citation. It breaks down as:
- **BGE** = Official collection of Federal Supreme Court decisions (Bundesgerichtsentscheide)
- **141** = Volume number
- **III** = Chamber designation (Civil Law, Third Division)
- **569** = Decision number

The citation resolves to a real decision from December 16, 2015, concerning tenant law (quartierübliche Mietzinse / loyers en vigueur dans la région). 

In French, the same decision is cited as **ATF 141 III 569**, and in Italian as **DTF 141 III 569**—all three forms are equivalent depending on the language context.
